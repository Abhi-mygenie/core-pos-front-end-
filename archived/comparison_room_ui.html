Collect Bill changes:
① Sticky header total: ₹305 → ₹4,305 (now includes room balance).
② NEW "Room" collapsible block at the very top of the bill summary (above Transferred Orders). Expanded view shows Room Charge / Advance Paid / Balance.
③ Existing "Transferred Orders" + "Room Service" rows unchanged in shape; their order is unchanged.
④ The single "Total" row is replaced by a NEW Grand-Total Stack: Food Total + Transferred Total + Room Balance → GRAND TOTAL. Lines auto-hide when 0 (so a non-room dine-in checkout shows only Food Total → GRAND TOTAL — no regression).
⑤ Cash-Received quick-amount chips reflect the new combined total (₹4,305 / ₹4,400 / ₹4,500).
⑥ Pay button: Checkout ₹305 → Checkout ₹4,305.
⑦ Architecture rule L2 still holds: SC %, GST %, discount % only operate on the Food Total row — they never touch the Room Balance row.
